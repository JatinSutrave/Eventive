Welcome to Mastershot - Where Stories Come to Life on the Silver Screen!


Mastershot is not just a college club; it's a creative hub where aspiring filmmakers gather to showcase their talent and passion for storytelling.As a member of Mastershot, you'll be immersed in a world of lights, cameras, and action. Our club provides a collaborative environment where students with diverse backgrounds and interests in filmmaking come together to create compelling and thought-provoking short films. From writing captivating scripts to capturing breathtaking visuals and editing masterpieces, Mastershot offers a holistic filmmaking experience.

Stay connected with Mastershot to learn about upcoming events, workshops, and opportunities to showcase your work. Step into the world of filmmaking with Mastershot - where stories are brought to life, one frame at a time!
