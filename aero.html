Welcome to AERO - Taking Flight with Innovation and Engineering!


AERO is not just a college club; it's a passionate community of aviation enthusiasts dedicated to pushing the boundaries of aerospace engineering. Embark on a journey where dreams of flight become a reality, as we come together to design, build, and soar with our very own aircraft.Be a part of our club and immerse yourself in a world of cutting-edge technology, advanced materials, and precision engineering. From conceptualizing and prototyping to testing and refining, AERO offers an invaluable opportunity to gain practical skills and insights that go beyond the confines of the classroom.

Stay connected with AERO to discover upcoming events, projects, and opportunities to be a part of this extraordinary journey. Let your passion for aviation soar high with AERO - where dreams take flight!
